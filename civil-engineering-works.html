CIVIL ENGINEERING WORK

Landsmann Services Limited is fully registered with various States and Federal Ministries of Works, the Department of Petroleum Resources (DPR), Nigerian Maritime Administration and Safety Agency (NIMASA) and Nigerian Ports Authority (NPA) to offer construction and engineering services. We pride ourselves on being a fledgling Construction outfit with state-of-the art machineries, experienced Engineers, Surveyors, Builders and Quantity Surveyors qualified to successfully carry out execution of all works such as Roads, Buildings, Pipelines, Water Reservoirs, Concrete Jetties, Canalization etc. Our services meet API and ISO 9001 Quality Assurance standards to ensure that our clients receive the highest quality services provided in Nigeria.
